# Nature Organic (S12) — SaaS Full Landing Page Design Prompt

## Objective
Design a **full SaaS landing page** using the **Nature Organic (S12)** style. The interface should feel grounded, human, and trustworthy—inspired by natural materials and organic systems—while remaining **clear, performant, and conversion-ready** for a modern SaaS product.

---

## Inputs
- Style: S12 — Nature Organic
- Industry: SaaS
- Use: Full Landing Page

---

## Assumptions
- B2B or mission-driven SaaS (climate, health, productivity, analytics)
- Primary CTA: Start Free Trial
- Secondary CTA: Request Demo
- All metrics, logos, and testimonials are placeholders
- Subtle texture and organic shapes allowed (CSS/SVG)
- Implementation via modern CSS / Tailwind

---

## Style DNA (S12 — Nature Organic)

### Visual Philosophy
- Human-first, calm, and grounded
- Soft geometry inspired by nature
- Trust through warmth and clarity

### Palette Strategy
- Light neutral backgrounds (linen, sand)
- Earth tones (green, clay, sky)
- Avoid harsh contrast

### Typography Direction
- Friendly sans-serif or humanist type
- Comfortable reading rhythm
- Moderate hierarchy

### Radius Policy
- Soft radius (10–14px)

### Shadow Policy
- Soft, diffused shadows
- Low elevation only

### Border / Line Language
- Rounded strokes
- Low-contrast outlines

### Pattern / Texture
- Subtle grain, paper, or organic noise
- Very low opacity

### Motion Philosophy
- Gentle, natural
- Ease-in-out transitions
- No sharp or sudden motion

---

## Industry Constraints & Trust Modules (SaaS)

### Required Trust Modules (Placeholders)
- Usage metrics (teams, regions)
- Partner or integration logos (muted)
- Privacy & reliability messaging (reassuring tone)
